Careers

Team Member

Join our crew and help serve smiles every day. Flexible hours and great benefits.

  • Greet customers and take orders

  • Maintain cleanliness and safety standards

Shift Manager

Lead a team, manage shifts, and ensure top-notch service. Leadership experience preferred.

  • Supervise team members
  • Handle customer concerns
  • Ensure smooth shift operations

Cook

Prepare delicious food in a fast-paced environment. Training provided.

  • Keep kitchen clean and organized
